The locked tomb

“Nine for the Tomb, and all that was lost....”

Hey book lovers! If you're in the mood for something fresh and thrilling, let me introduce you to the first book in The Locked Tomb series: "Gideon the Ninth", by Tamsyn Muir. This book is only getting more and more attention, and for good reason! It's an intoxicating blend of sci-fi, fantasy and mystery that'll keep you hooked from page one. Here's why you should add it to your reading list asap!

A wild premise...

Picture this: a world full of necromancers... but they are in space? Sounds intriguing, right? Meet Gideon Nav, our bold and proud protagonist, who finds herself teamed up with her former rival, the mysterious Harrowhark Nonagesimus. The world Muir creates is captivating, filled with secrets and political conflicts that will pull you in.

Characters You'll Love!

Gideon is such a relatable character. She's tough, witty, and not afraid to speak her mind. Her dynamic with Harrowhark is a rivalry turned partnership. It is both funny and beautiful how their dynamic evolves trough the story. Plus, the supporting cast is just as vibrant, adding layers and complexity to the story.

Humor That Hits Just Right!

One of my favorite things about this book is its humor. Muir's sharp wit shines through, making for some truly hilarious moments that perfectly balance the darker themes. The dialogue is snappy and engaging, keeping the tone lively even when the stakes are high.

Deep Themes to Ponder...

But it's not just fun and games. Gideon the Ninth explores heavy themes like loyalty, identity, and the complexities of power. It makes you think about what it means to wield authority and the responsibilities that come with it. Muir really dives into the nuances of relationships, especially those forged in conflict and competition.

Queer Representation Done Right!

Let's talk representation! This book proudly features queer characters, and the romance between Gideon and Harrowhark is beautifully crafted. The book is filled with powerful women that feel real and alive. The characters come in all kind of shapes and ethnicities. Muir's approaches to these themes showcases a spectrum of identities that feel real and relatable.

A Plot Full of Surprises...

The twists and turns in Gideon the Ninth are jaw-dropping. Just when you think you've figured things out, Muir throws in a surprise that leaves you breathless. The combination of mystery and suspense makes it nearly impossible to put the book down—trust me, you'll want to binge-read!

Final Thoughts:

The world is full of unpredictable plots that will have you mindblown when the last puzzle piece falls in place. Gideon the Ninth is a breath of fresh air. Tamsyn Muir has created a amazing universe filled with complex characters, humor, and thought-provoking themes. Whether you're a die-hard fan of fantasy and sci-fi, or just looking for something new, this book will satisfy your craving. So grab your copy and step into the Ninth House—you won't regret it!

Happy reading!
